Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

How long does a typical Odoo implementation take?

While every project is unique, our streamlined process allows us to get most businesses live with core Odoo functionality in just a few weeks, not months. Complex projects with heavy customization may take longer, but our focus is always on speed and results.

Is Odoo suitable for a small business?

Absolutely. Odoo is incredibly scalable. You can start with just one or two apps (like Invoicing and CRM) at a very low cost and add more modules as your business grows. This makes it a perfect fit for ambitious small and medium-sized businesses.

What kind of support do you offer after we go live?

Our partnership does not end at launch. We offer ongoing support packages that include technical assistance, strategic advice, and help with optimizations to ensure you're getting the most out of your ERP investment as you continue to grow.
